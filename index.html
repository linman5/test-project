Lorna Inman

Virtual Reality Artist.

Hi! Welcome to my website.
I'm Lorna, a Virtual Reality Artist, VR facilitator and XR enthusiast. I hand-paint immersive worlds from scratch to create memorable experiences for brands, events, conferences and have dabbled in VR live performing. My portfolio consists of client work, my own current art practice, and my university work. Take a glance at my video reel following the button above or have a closer look at each of my projects below!

I'm a graduate of BA Art & Design (International) from the University of Leeds and I did a study year abroad at the Accademia di Belle Arti di Brera in Milan, Italy. My interest and love for exciting, immersive, emotion-filled expereinces of art has lead me to where I am today, and with this I have become enticed by the notion that immersive environments have the potential to elicit positive emotional states.

Ninety One

Investec Asset Management have rebranded to Ninety One. This immersive timeline pathway takes the viewer through the development of their brand and the cultural influences that made them who they are from their nascent beginnings til now.

Waterfall

Waterfall is a painting created from imagination, experimenting with creating water textures and lighting as part of my artistic practice.

Google Automation Summit

I performed live on stage at the Google Automation Summit for 150 guests, creating an environment that described the topics that were going to be spoken about at the event.

Google Finance Week

Entertaining Google's Finance Week attendees with Tilt Brush, I created two drawings as templates of the Google logos for guests to embellish as they wish, facillitating many of their first tries using VR.

Forlorn Park

My final project for my Degree Show, completing my Art & Design degree. It explores the theme of urban parks and their potential insight into the human - nature relationship.

Cave

This painting was an experiment of creating atmospheric light textures in Tilt Brush.
